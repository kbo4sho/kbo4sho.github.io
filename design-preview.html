Selected Work · 01

Agentic creative tooling at Epsilon

Epsilon · 2023–Present · Staff Software Engineer

AI Creative Production Platform

No-code creative ad tooling with a homegrown agentic orchestration loop across tiered models.

The problem

Creative production didn't scale. Every ad variation was a human-in-the-loop craft act. We needed a system where a small team of creators could produce thousands of variations per day — without sacrificing brand fidelity.

The approach

I architected a homegrown agentic orchestration loop over tiered Gemini models, with humans in the loop at decision points. Production-data bridges (like real-time font-ID lookups) keep AI outputs tied to the actual brand system rather than generically "looking good."

The leverage move

I built a reusable eval skill that every AI feature in the org now plugs into. The skill grades its own outputs and self-improves on a recursive loop. Invest once in a narrow substrate; compound leverage across every AI feature downstream.

What's next

Leading an agent-first refactor of the platform — migrating deterministic orchestration into fully agentic workflows, with the eval harness as the safety rail.
