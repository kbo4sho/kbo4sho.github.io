Work

Tools for Creative Teams

Our creative team is tasked to scale ad production across hundreds of formats and personalization rules, I helped design and build a custom tool that empowered designers to create flexible, intelligent templates without writing code.

I led the architecture and buildout of the tool, working closely with product, UX, and creatives SMEs to understand the needs and ensure we were building the right thing and aligned on the expectations. We built features like dynamic cropping, smart layout rules, and an inline preview of how an ad would adapt across sizes and animations. What used to take hours per creative format now takes minutes. This project has gone from concept to production and is now used daily by internal teams to ship campaigns for major brands, with thousands of ad variations generated each week from just a handful of templates.

Scalable Design Systems

Between 2017 and 2021, I helped build and grow our internal design system into a foundational part of the product development process. As a UX engineer, I focused on bridging the gap between design and engineering—turning high-level patterns into accessible, flexible components that scaled across teams and platforms.

I led frontend implementation for core components, collaborated closely with designers to evolve the system’s visual language, and maintained the documentation site that helped teams onboard quickly. Beyond code, I hosted office hours, supported adoption across a range of product teams, and shaped design principles that balanced creative freedom with consistency. Our system went from an optional library to an essential part of the workflow.
